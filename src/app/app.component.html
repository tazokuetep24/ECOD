Welcome to the Renewable Energy Chart Observer RECO

Our Goal

With these Charts, we intend to inform people living in Germany of the amount of Renewable Energy that is being produced in Germany, as a percentage of the total amount of energy produced.
You can either view the Forecast of the energy produced today, or view the daily proportion for the last 10 years.
